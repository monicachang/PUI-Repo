This project imitates the work of Duane Michals, an American photographer and innovator of the last century, most known for his cinematic sequences, use of written words, and often surrealist photographs. The first photograph attempts to imitate his photo titled "The Unfortunate Man" and the second photograph attempts to imitate a captioned photograph from one of his books, Take One and See Mt. Fujiyama and Other Stories.
